Principal's Message
A piece of green chunk of beauty is always caught in the camera when the drone of an inquisitive beauty-seeker starts flying for beauty in the northern part of Bangladesh. This is the beauty of success, the glamour of achievements, the revelation of patriotism. This is Rajshahi Cadet College. Standing on the bank of the mighty Padma, in distance of twenty-eight kilometers from Rajshahi city, this glorious institution commenced its journey on 11 February, 1966. On her motherly lap of nourishment and with the promise of dedication, Rajshahi Cadet College ensures the successful germination of flourished talents every year. Here, the unending cordial attachment and the irresistible adventurous efforts of the authority, cadets and employees have kept the victorious flag of this prestigious institution fluttering on. The promising passed out cadets of Rajshahi Cadet College are serving in the national and international domains with their supreme dedication in multi-dimensional professional premises. In a word, the professional and personal flourishment of the learners of this Institution have brought the testimony of everlasting glory to their sacred alma mater of knowledge and wisdom.
Rajshahi Cadet College has achieved illustrious status nationally and internationally in rendering training to the cadets with a great vision for a glorious future. As a foremost provider of quality education, we are honestly committed to meet the needs of our cadets who experience contemporary and student-oriented learning environment. Here, the young cadets are taught and trained by the highly qualified and well trained academicians and military staffs. We take pride on providing suitable supports to our cadets in order to make them good human beings.
Furthermore, Old Rajshahi Cadets Association (ORCA) has already won hearts of countless people and earned laurels at home and abroad through their various philanthropic activities. Those benevolent activities include the maintenance of foster homes known as 'ORCA Homes' for distressed and orphaned children, sheltering of the landless and homeless people at cluster homes known as 'ORCA Palli' , distribution of Relief and Warm Clothes among distressed people, organization of voluntary Blood Donation Programs, etc.
We pass on true education and strong ethical values into the deeper insight of our cadets which enrich and enlighten the personality, self-respect, integrity, loyalty and patriotism of the cadets.
I hope all the best of this prestigious institution. Let us stay together in the splendid journey of this renowned institution of the country .
Dr. A F M Murtahan Billah, Principal, RCC
